Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to common questions about the City of Windsor Open Data Portal.

  • How can I download datasets? Navigate to the "Data Catalog" page and select a dataset to download in the format you prefer.
  • Who can upload data? City staff and administrators have the ability to upload and manage datasets.
  • How often is the data updated? Datasets are updated regularly based on city operations and events.
